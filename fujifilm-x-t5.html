Fujifilm X-T5

The Fujifilm X-T5 is a compact, lightweight mirrorless camera built with a “photography-first” design, combining a classic body with advanced fifth- generation imaging technology to deliver high- resolution image quality and versatile shooting performance.

Specs and Features:

  1. 40.2MP APS-C sensor + X-Processor 5
  2. 5-axis IBIS (up to 7 stops)
  3. AI subject-detection autofocus
  4. 6.2K video recording
  5. 3-way tilting LCD, high-res EVF
  6. 19 Film Simulation modes
  7. Weather-resistant body, dual SD slots
